Our Sectors

Expertise Across the Energy Sector

We have deep experience working with each sector of the UK energy industry, understanding the specific grid code requirements, technical standards, and commercial drivers that shape each market segment.

Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS)

The UK BESS market has grown rapidly, with hundreds of MWs of storage now operational or in development. We support developers throughout the project lifecycle with comprehensive modelling services.

  • Grid-forming inverter modelling and validation
  • Frequency response services (FFR, Dynamic Modular)
  • Synthetic inertia and RoCoF studies
  • Black start capability assessment
  • Capacity Market technical submissions
  • Connection offer response and negotiation

Solar Photovoltaics

Utility-scale solar continues to expand across the UK, with new connections requiring rigorous technical assessment to ensure compatibility with the distribution and transmission networks.

  • LV and HV connection studies for solar farms
  • Inverter trip settings and fault ride-through
  • G98/G99 application support for DNOs
  • Power quality assessment (harmonic, flicker)
  • Export limitation studies and reactive power
  • Panel degradation modelling over project lifetime

Wind Energy

From onshore wind farms in Scotland to offshore developments in the North Sea, we provide the technical modelling required to bring wind projects through connection and into operation.

  • Onshore and offshore wind turbine modelling
  • Grid code compliance studies (ERC/1, ERC/2)
  • Power system stabiliser tuning
  • Wind farm collector system design support
  • Cape-Wellhead generator modelling
  • Offshore reactive power platform studies

Transmission & Distribution Networks

Working alongside National Grid, Distribution Network Operators, and Independent Network Operators to ensure the UK grid remains stable, secure, and able to accommodate new generation.

  • National Grid connection application studies
  • DNO connection offers and offer compliance
  • Network reinforcement optioneering
  • Short circuit level assessment
  • Protection coordination and settings
  • System operability and boundary studies

Industrial & Commercial

Large industrial sites, data centres, and commercial developments require robust electrical infrastructure. We provide modelling and assessment services for new connections and upgrades.

  • Industrial site connection studies
  • On-site generation and CHP modelling
  • Network capacity assessment
  • Power factor correction studies
  • Standby generator and UPS assessment
  • Emissions compliance modelling

Electric Vehicle Infrastructure

The rapid growth of EV charging networks creates new demands on the electricity network. We support chargepoint operators and developers with technical assessment services.

  • Rapid charging hub connection studies
  • Distribution network impact assessment
  • Multiple EV charger aggregation modelling
  • Demand side response capability assessment
  • Grid reinforcement cost-benefit analysis
  • V2G (Vehicle-to-Grid) feasibility studies
