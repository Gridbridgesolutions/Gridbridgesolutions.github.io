What We Do

Types of Projects

From initial feasibility through to post-connection compliance, we support projects at every stage of the development lifecycle.

01
Renewable Energy

Wind Farm Connection Studies

Comprehensive technical assessment for onshore and offshore wind farm connections to the UK transmission and distribution networks.

  • Full scope connection application studies
  • Grid code compliance (ERC/1, ERC/2, P28)
  • Fault ride-through and protection studies
  • Power system stabiliser design and tuning
  • Collector network and cable rating studies
  • Connection offer review and negotiation
02
Energy Storage

Battery Storage Modelling

Detailed modelling and simulation for BESS projects, from feasibility through to operational optimisation and services provision.

  • Grid-forming inverter modelling and validation
  • Frequency response services (FFR, DMS, BM Participation)
  • Synthetic inertia and RoCoF studies
  • Black start capability assessment
  • Capacity Market and Balancing Mechanism studies
  • Long-duration storage assessment
03
Solar Energy

Solar Farm Technical Studies

Technical assessment for utility-scale solar PV connections, including G99 compliance and power quality verification.

  • LV and HV connection studies
  • Inverter trip settings optimisation
  • Fault ride-through compliance studies
  • Power quality (harmonic, flicker, voltage) assessment
  • G99 application support and DNO liaison
  • Reactive power capability assessment
04
Industrial

Industrial Connection Studies

Technical assessment for large industrial loads, on-site generation, and grid connection upgrades.

  • Industrial site connection offers
  • On-site CHP and generator modelling
  • Network capacity and reinforcement studies
  • Power factor correction and harmonics
  • Standby generator and UPS assessment
  • Emissions and grid code compliance
05
Transmission

Grid Infrastructure Assessment

Technical studies for transmission network connections, reinforcements, and infrastructure projects.

  • National Grid connection application support
  • Network reinforcement optioneering
  • Short circuit level assessment
  • Protection coordination studies
  • System boundary and operability studies
  • HVDC and FACTS modelling
06
Model Validation

OEM Model Development

Development and validation of manufacturer-specific models for wind turbines, inverters, and storage systems.

  • WTG manufacturer model development
  • Inverter and BESS controller modelling
  • Model validation against site measurements
  • DNO/TO model acceptance support
  • Generic model development and certification
  • Real code compliance verification
