Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What information do you need to provide a quote?

Typically we need: project location and capacity, connection voltage level (LV/HV/EHV), whether you have a connection offer already, and a brief description of the technical studies required. If you have a connection offer or DNO correspondence, sharing that helps us understand the scope faster.

How long do typical studies take?

Standard studies are typically completed within 2-4 weeks from receipt of data. Complex projects or those requiring extensive model building may take longer. We always agree timelines upfront and keep you informed throughout.

Do you work with projects outside the UK?

Our primary focus is the UK energy sector, but we do work on projects in Ireland and occasionally further afield. UK grid codes and standards are our core expertise, but we can adapt our approach for other jurisdictions.

Can you support with DNO or National Grid liaison?

Yes. We regularly support clients in discussions with DNOs and National Grid, including attending technical meetings, responding to information requests, and helping negotiate connection offers. We understand the utility perspective and can help navigate the connection process.

What software platforms do you use?

We work across all major power system modelling platforms including DIgSILENT PowerFactory, PSCAD, PSS®E, and CDEGS. We select the most appropriate tool for each study type, and can work with models in any of these formats.

Do you offer ongoing modelling support contracts?

Yes. Many of our clients have ongoing relationships with us for modelling support throughout a project's lifecycle. We offer retainer arrangements for developers and operators who need regular access to power system expertise without maintaining full-time in-house capability.
