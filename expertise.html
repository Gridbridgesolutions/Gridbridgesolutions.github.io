Software Platforms

Industry-Standard Tools

Each platform offers distinct strengths for different aspects of power system analysis.

DIgSILENT
PowerFactory

Commercial-grade power system analysis software widely used across the UK and European transmission networks. PowerFactory excels in balanced and unbalanced load flow, fault analysis, RMS/EMT simulation, and reliability studies. It's the preferred choice for National Grid and Distribution Network Operator studies due to its comprehensive protection modelling and automated reporting capabilities.

Load FlowFault AnalysisRMS/EMT SimulationProtection Co-ordinationReliability StudiesDynamic Stability
PSCAD

Industry-leading electromagnetic transient simulation environment. PSCAD is essential for detailed studies of fault ride-through, converter control interactions, and hardware-in-the-loop testing. Particularly valuable for renewable energy connections where understanding high-frequency dynamics and control system behaviour is critical for successful DNO/TO submissions.

EMT SimulationFault Ride-ThroughConverter ControlHIL TestingWind/Solar ModellingGrid Code Compliance
PSS®E

Renowned in transmission system analysis and extensively used by National Grid and major generators. Its strength lies in large-scale network studies, cascading failure analysis, and long-term stability assessments. The platform's extensive library of generator models and automatic transmission network equivalent creation make it indispensable for complex grid connection studies.

Transmission StudiesCascading FailureLong-Term StabilityNetwork EquivalentsInterconnection StudiesVoltage Stability
CDEGS

Specialised environment for grounding analysis, electromagnetic interference studies, and cable system modelling. Essential for substation grounding grid design, pipeline interference assessment, and AC/DC interference studies. Comprehensive electromagnetic field calculation capabilities make it the go-to tool for projects involving complex earthing arrangements or proximity to existing infrastructure.

Grounding AnalysisEMI StudiesCable ModellingInterference AssessmentSubstation DesignAC/DC Interference
What We Do

Core Capabilities

Beyond platform expertise, we bring deep technical knowledge across all aspects of power system modelling.

Grid Connection Studies

Full scope studies for NGET and DNO connection applications, including fault level, load flow, and protection assessments.

Renewable Energy Modelling

Detailed modelling of wind, solar, and battery storage with accurate converter and control system representation.

Protection & Co-ordination

Protection relay settings, selectivity studies, and coordination analysis for transmission and distribution networks.

Power Quality Analysis

Harmonic assessment, voltage flicker analysis, and power quality compliance verification for grid connections.

BESS & Storage Studies

Performance modelling, frequency response studies, and grid services assessment for battery energy storage systems.

Thermal Rating Assessment

Line and cable thermal ratings, real-time monitoring integration, and climate-adjusted capacity analysis.

Network Reinforcement

Reinforcement option identification, comparative analysis, and optimal solution development for constrained networks.

HVDC & FACTS

Modelling of voltage source converters, HVDC links, and flexible AC transmission systems for grid applications.
